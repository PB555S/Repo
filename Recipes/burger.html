Delicious Homemade Burgers

Sink your teeth into juicy, flavorful homemade burgers made with fresh ground beef, herbs, and your favorite toppings. Perfectly grilled and served on a toasted bun, they're a classic comfort food everyone loves.

This recipe walks you through every stepâ€”from forming the perfect patties to customizing with cheese, sauces, and vegetables. Whether you're cooking for family or friends, these burgers never disappoint.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Ketchup and mustard
  1. Toast the burger buns lightly in a pan or toaster.
  2. Cook the burger patty on a grill or frying pan until fully done.
  3. Place the patty on the bottom half of the bun.
  4. Add a slice of cheese on top of the patty (optional).
  5. Layer with lettuce, tomato, onions, and pickles as desired.
  6. Spread sauces like ketchup, mustard, or mayo on the top bun.
  7. Close the bun and serve the burger hot.
