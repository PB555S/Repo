Delicious Homemade Steak

Sink your teeth into juicy, flavorful homemade Steak>

This recipe walks you through every of making a delicious steak at home>

Ingredients

  • 1 ribeye steak (or cut of your choice)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Fresh rosemary or thyme (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Steps to Cook the Steak

  1. Take the steak out of the fridge 30 minutes before cooking to bring it to room temperature.
  2. Season both sides generously with salt and black pepper.
  3. Heat a pan over high heat and add olive oil.
  4. Place the steak in the hot pan and sear for 2â€“3 minutes per side (or more depending on thickness).
  5. Lower heat, add butter, garlic, and herbs to the pan.
  6. Spoon the melted butter over the steak for added flavor (basting).
  7. Remove the steak and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.
